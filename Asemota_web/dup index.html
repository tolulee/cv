About

I am currently a junior concentrating in Computer Science and Biomolecular Science at New York University ! I was first introduced to the interconnectedness of these diverse fields as a Student Spotlight speaker at the Inspired @ Google event. Interests: Artificial Intelligence • Robotics • Graphic Design • Web & App Development • Augmented Reality • Traveling
